# Online-Food-Delivery-Analysis
Online food delivery analysis focuses on understanding customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, and revenue trends to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
Key Insights from the Analysis

Order & Revenue Performance

Tracks total orders and total revenue to measure overall business growth.

Average order value helps understand customer spending behavior.

Customer & Demand Patterns

Identifies peak vs non-peak hours to optimize delivery resources.

City and cuisine analysis shows regional food preferences.

Delivery Efficiency

Average delivery time highlights operational performance.

Delivery ratings indicate customer satisfaction with logistics.

Restaurant Performance

Restaurant ratings help identify top and underperforming partners.

Enables quality control and restaurant onboarding decisions.

Payment & Ordering Behavior

Payment mode analysis reveals customer payment preferences.

Helps in optimizing payment offers and checkout experience.

Cancellation Analysis

Cancellation rate and reasons help detect operational issues.

Comparing delivered vs cancelled orders shows service reliability.
